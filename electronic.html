ZEDD

Anton Zaslavski better known by his stage name Zedd is a Russian-German Grammy Award winning musician, music producer, and DJ. He primarily produces in the electro house genre, but has branched out, drawing influences from progressive house, dubstep, and classical music.

MARTIN GARRIX

Martijn Gerard Garritsen, better known by his stage name Martin Garrix, is a Dutch DJ, record producer and musician. He is currently ranked number 3 on DJ Mag. He founded and owns the record label STMPD RCRDS.

DIPLO

Thomas Wesley Pentz, better known by his stage name Diplo, is an American DJ, record producer, rapper and songwriter based in Los Angeles, California.
