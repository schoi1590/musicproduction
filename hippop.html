OLD SCHOOL

Old school hip hop (also spelled “old skool“) describes the earliest commercially recorded hip hop music (approximately from 1979–1984), and the music in the period preceding it from which it was directly descended (see Roots of hip hop).

REAL HIP-HOP

In rap and hip hop, there is always this divide on what is “Real”. Other genres, like rock for example people will just call a band bad or garbage and call it a day. Hip hop however, there is a whole different spectrum of being “real hip hop” and what just isn’t.
